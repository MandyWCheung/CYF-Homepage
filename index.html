career

I currently work for a small and remote recruitment. I began as an Intern who was pushed towards Sales. I didn't enjoy Sales and quickly gravitated towards solving problems with the company's software.

Fast forward to 2022, I'm now the Operations Coordinator which means I wear a good few hats! My skills come in handy for technical and admin support, data and website management, and training people on my team on how to use the CRM and WordPress.

I love working within a small team as I have the flexibility to tinker with our CRM behind the scenes. However, I can't see myself staying in a job with little career advancement or growth opportunities for the next 5 years. This is one of my "Why's" for embarking on a journey into tech.

dreams

Why learn to code? Back in the days of dial-up broadband, I was completely hooked on this website called Neopets. I remember being able to edit my profile page with HTML (although, I didn't know it at the time!).

With no coding role models in my life, I didn't pursue this curiosity and instead took up engineering at university. Today, I find myself questioning my career, I'd love to do something that I love. Where do I want to be in 5 years time? What can I contribute to? What kind of role model would my younger self have needed? And can I be that person?
